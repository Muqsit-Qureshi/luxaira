Our Story

From a visionary idea to a global luxury brand

Crafting Luxury Since 2010

Luxaira was born from a passion for creating exceptional beauty products that combine the finest natural ingredients with scientific innovation. Our founder, Isabella Laurent, envisioned a brand that would redefine luxury cosmetics by prioritizing both efficacy and ethical practices.

What began as a small boutique in Geneva has blossomed into an internationally recognized brand, yet we've never lost sight of our core values: quality, sustainability, and innovation. Each product still receives the same meticulous attention it did in our early days.

Today, we continue to push boundaries with cutting-edge research while maintaining our commitment to environmentally responsible practices. Our state-of-the-art facility in Switzerland is powered by renewable energy, and we've committed to becoming carbon neutral by 2025.

Isabella Laurent, Founder & CEO

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped Luxaira into the brand it is today

2010

Foundation

Luxaira was founded in Geneva, Switzerland with a vision to create luxury, effective skincare that respects both people and planet.

2012

First Retail Store

Opened our flagship store in Geneva, featuring our complete collection of skincare and makeup products.

2013

First Product Line

Launched our signature Luminous Glow Collection, which quickly became an editor's favorite in luxury beauty magazines.

2015

Leaping Bunny Certification

Received official certification from Leaping Bunny, confirming our commitment to cruelty-free practices.

2016

International Expansion

Expanded to markets in Asia and North America, bringing our Swiss-formulated luxury to beauty enthusiasts worldwide.

2018

Sustainable Packaging Initiative

Introduced fully recyclable and biodegradable packaging across our entire product line.

2019

Sustainability Initiative

Committed to 100% sustainable sourcing and introduced our innovative refill program to reduce packaging waste.

2021

Global Ambassador Program

Launched our ambassador program with influential advocates for sustainable beauty worldwide.

2022

Biotechnology Innovation

Pioneered the use of plant stem cell technology in our new Age-Defying collection, winning multiple industry awards.

2023

Global Recognition

Received the prestigious Beauty Innovation Award for our commitment to combining luxury, efficacy, and sustainability.
